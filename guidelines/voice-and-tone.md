## Tone By Channel

| Channel | Tone | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Professional, clear, opinionated | Avoid corporate filler |
| Instagram | Visual, direct, memorable | Keep slide text short |
| TikTok/Reels/Shorts | Fast, concrete, spoken | Hook in first seconds |
| Blog | Helpful, structured, source-backed | Build authority |

## Writing Preferences

- Prefer concrete examples over generic advice.
- Prefer short paragraphs for social copy.
- Use Portuguese by default.
- Avoid empty hype.
