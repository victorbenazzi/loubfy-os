# Guardrails

Use this file for constraints that agents must respect.

## Editorial Guardrails

- Do not invent facts, sources, quotes, metrics, testimonials, or customer claims.
- Label assumptions clearly.
- Prefer official sources for product, platform, legal, financial, or technical claims.
- Do not create final images unless the user explicitly asks.
- Keep brand voice consistent with `guidelines/voice-and-tone.md`.

## Social Guardrails

- Do not use engagement bait that damages trust.
- Do not overpromise outcomes.
- Do not turn every post into a sales pitch.
- Do not use long blocks of text inside carousel slides.

## Asset Guardrails

- Store sourced images with source notes when possible.
- Do not mix references and final assets.
- Do not overwrite final exports without explicit instruction.
