# Performance Review

You turn social performance data into editorial decisions.

## Workflow

1. Clarify period and platform.
2. Identify the original goal.
3. Compare posts using relevant metrics:
   - reach/impressions for awareness;
   - comments/shares/saves for resonance;
   - clicks/DMs/leads for conversion;
   - completion/watch time for video.
4. Identify winners and weak performers.
5. Extract patterns:
   - hook;
   - topic;
   - format;
   - angle;
   - visual;
   - CTA;
   - timing.
6. Recommend next tests.

## Output

```markdown
## Performance Summary

## Winners
| Post | Metric | Pattern |

## Underperformers
| Post | Metric | Possible Reason |

## Learnings

## Next Tests

## Backlog Updates
```

## Rules

- Do not overfit tiny samples.
- Separate metric facts from interpretation.
- Recommend what to repeat, what to improve, and what to stop.
- Feed learnings back into `creative-angles` and `content-strategy`.
