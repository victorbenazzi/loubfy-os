# Content Scoring

Score each content idea from 1 to 10.

| Factor | Weight | Question |
|---|---:|---|
| Audience impact | 40% | Does this solve a frequent or emotionally strong audience problem? |
| Brand/content fit | 30% | Does this connect naturally to the creator, brand, product, or POV? |
| Distribution potential | 20% | Can this become a strong post, carousel, video, thread, or collab? |
| Production effort | 10% | Can we make it well with available time and assets? |

Formula:

```text
score = impact*0.4 + fit*0.3 + distribution*0.2 + effort*0.1
```

Score production effort higher when the idea is easier to execute.
