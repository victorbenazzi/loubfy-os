# Hook Patterns

## Clear Promise

- How to [outcome] without [pain].
- The simplest way to [outcome].
- If you struggle with [problem], start here.

## Contrarian

- Everyone says [X]. The real issue is [Y].
- [Common advice] is not wrong, but it is incomplete.
- Stop trying to [surface tactic]. Fix [root cause].

## Story

- I used to think [old belief]. Then [event] changed it.
- Last week, [specific moment] revealed something important.
- A client asked me [question]. The answer became a framework.

## Mistake

- The mistake is not [obvious thing]. It is [deeper thing].
- Most people skip this step.
- This is why your [thing] feels harder than it should.

## Comparison

- [A] is for [use case]. [B] is for [use case].
- The difference between [A] and [B] is not what people think.

- Use this 3-step process.
- Save this checklist.
- Here is the exact structure.
